Description

Sport legging shorts for women are comfortable and practical, made of high quality elastic fabric, perfect for gym, home, street and outdoor activities. Women's high-waisted legging shorts have a sewn wide elastic band that hides problem areas, preventing them from slipping off the waist. Shorts at the bottom do not overtighten the legs due to the elastic seam. Stretch moderately, do not catch, do not roll, do not stretch, do not shine through, do not shed, pleasant to the body and to the touch, allow the skin to breathe.
